Section 01

Client Responsibilities

The success of this engagement depends on active, timely participation from the Client. By entering this Agreement, the Client commits to the following:

  • Providing all required content, brand assets, access credentials, and materials within 5 business days of each request, unless a different date is agreed in writing.
  • Designating a single point of contact with authority to provide feedback and give Approvals on behalf of the Client organisation.
  • Responding to Studio communications and feedback requests within 5 business days. Delayed responses that affect the project timeline do not entitle the Client to a fee reduction.
  • Ensuring all materials supplied (text, images, logos, trademarks) are owned by or licensed to the Client, free of third-party claims.
  • Consolidating all feedback into a single, clear submission per revision round, rather than issuing feedback across multiple messages or stages.
Timeline dependency. Project timelines in the Proposal are contingent on the Client meeting the responsibilities above. Delays attributable to the Client may cause timeline shifts. A hold exceeding 21 calendar days may incur a project re-engagement fee to reschedule Studio resources.
Section 02

Revisions & Change Management

The number of included revision rounds is stated in the Proposal (2 rounds). The following definitions and conditions apply to all revision activity:

📋

What Is a Revision

  • One consolidated set of feedback per stage
  • Submitted in writing via agreed channel
  • Specific, actionable, and within brief
🔄

What Is NOT a Revision

  • Change to layout concept or colour direction
  • New functionality not in the original brief
  • Multiple feedback emails per stage
➕

Extra Rounds

  • Billed at the Studio's standard rate
  • Agreed in writing before commencement
  • See Proposal for per-round rate

Requests that materially change the original brief — including concept direction, core page structure, or feature set — are treated as scope changes, not revisions, and are subject to a Change Order (see below).

Section 03

Scope Boundaries

Work is limited to what is described in the Proposal. Items not listed there are outside scope.

Outside Scope — Not Included

  • Content writing, copyediting, or SEO strategy
  • Paid advertising setup or management
  • Third-party software licences or platform fees
  • Ongoing maintenance beyond the support period
  • Social media graphics or ongoing content creation
  • Email marketing design or platform configuration

Change Orders

Any request to expand scope is handled via a written Change Order: a short document stating the additional work, timeline impact, and cost. Both parties must sign before new work begins. Change Orders become part of this Agreement.

Section 04

Intellectual Property & Ownership

  • Final Files. Upon receipt of full payment, the Studio assigns to the Client all rights, title, and interest in the Final Files produced specifically for this Project.
  • Working Files. Editable source files, design system frameworks, and reusable components remain the Studio's property unless their transfer is explicitly included in the Proposal and separately invoiced.
  • Third-party assets. Stock imagery, fonts, icon libraries, and software frameworks are subject to their own licences. The Client is responsible for acquiring any licences required for ongoing commercial use.
  • Portfolio rights. The Studio retains the right to display the completed work in its portfolio, case studies, and marketing materials, unless the Client requests confidentiality in writing prior to launch.
  • Pre-existing IP. Each party retains ownership of intellectual property created prior to or independently of this engagement.
Section 05

Payment Obligations

Payment follows the schedule stated in the Proposal. The following conditions govern all payments under this Agreement:

  • Work commences only upon receipt of the deposit payment. The Studio reserves the right to pause or withhold delivery until payments are brought current.
  • All invoices are payable within 7 days of the invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the legal maximum, whichever is less.
  • All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. The Client is responsible for any sales tax, VAT, or withholding taxes.
  • Fees quoted in the Proposal are valid for 14 days from issue. Acceptance after that period may be subject to revised pricing.
Non-payment. If payment is not received within 30 days of the due date, the Studio may suspend hosted deliverables, withhold Final Files, and pursue recovery of outstanding amounts including reasonable collection costs.
